# Ecommerce Sales Analysis for Elist

## Project Goal

**The goal is to empower stakeholders with a clear understanding of company performance, enabling data-driven decisions that improve day-to-day processes and enhance the customer experience worldwide**

By partnering with the Head of Operations, the project seeks to deliver targeted insights of the operational and financial data from 2019 to 2023 to four key departments:
- **Finance:** Analyzing revenue streams and order statuses to improve bottom-line forecasting
- **Sales & Marketing:** Evaluating the effectiveness of different marketing channels (SEO vs. Affiliate) to optimize customer acquisition costs
- **Product:** Identifying high-performing brands and products to refine inventory and selection
- **Operations:** Mapping geographic data to streamline global delivery and logistics

## Background

Founded in 2018, Elist is a rapidly growing global e-commerce retailer specialising in high-demand consumer electronics and tech hardware from industry giants like Apple, Samsung, and Lenovo (ThinkPad). Operating through both a web platform and a mobile app, the company leverages a multi-channel marketing strategy—incorporating SEO, email campaigns, and affiliate partnerships—to drive its international sales.

Despite its market presence, Elist’s primary challenge lies in its unrefined core dataset. While the company captures comprehensive data across orders, customer demographics, product categories, and global logistics, the information remains "messy" and underutilised.
